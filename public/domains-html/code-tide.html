// The Problem

The Sea Doesn't
Wait for Anyone.

Every morning, thousands of fishermen along India's coast set sail into the unknown — with no real-time weather alerts, no fair price for their catch, and no safety net if something goes wrong.

They are the backbone of a ₹2.5 lakh crore industry — yet they remain the most digitally invisible community in the country.

That's where you come in. As a student builder, you have the tools, the creativity, and now — the challenge — to change this.

⛈️
No Weather Alerts
Fishermen rely on word-of-mouth for sea conditions. Lives are lost every monsoon season.
💸
Unfair Pricing
Middlemen control fish auction prices. Fishermen earn a fraction of the true market value.
🆘
No SOS Systems
Vessels in distress have no reliable digital rescue coordination system in place.
🏦
Zero Credit Access
Fishermen can't access formal loans or insurance, leaving them in perpetual debt cycles.
// The Mission

One Coast.
Infinite Solutions.

🌊
Integrated Coastal Innovation
This challenge focuses on transforming the maritime ecosystem through a multi-dimensional approach. We are looking for Disaster Tech solutions like real-time cyclone alerts and IoT-based safety systems for GPS vessel tracking to protect lives at sea. To empower local economies, we encourage the development of FinTech platforms for transparent fish price discovery and Financial Inclusion tools that provide micro-loans and parametric insurance to coastal communities. Furthermore, we aim to bridge the digital divide using Conversational AI in vernacular languages like Kannada and Tamil. Beyond these tracks, we welcome Open Innovation in the broader Blue Economy—including aquaculture, marine biodiversity, and sustainable coastal tourism.
