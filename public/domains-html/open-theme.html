The Domain
With No
Domain.

Every other hackathon domain gives you a problem. This one gives you a blank canvas. Walk in with any idea — from any field, any industry, any corner of your curiosity — and build something the world hasn't seen yet.

Healthcare? Go for it. Gaming? Absolutely. Space tech? Yes. AR for street vendors? A legal AI for rural India? A mental health app for college students? All of it belongs here.

The only thing we're judging is: Does it solve a real problem? Is it bold? Can it scale? That's it. Everything else is up to you.

// What We Judge

Six Things
That Win.

01
🎯
Real Problem
Does your idea solve something that genuinely hurts people? The best hacks scratch real itches — not imaginary ones. Ground your idea in truth.
02
⚡
Bold Vision
Play big or go home. We don't want incremental improvements to existing apps. We want ideas that make judges say "why hasn't this been built yet?"
03
🔨
Working Build
A prototype that works — even partially — beats a perfect pitch deck every time. Show us something real. Click it. Demo it. Break it in front of us.
04
📈
Scale Potential
Can this reach 1,000 users? 1 million? Tell us who needs this and how you'd get it to them. Ambition is not arrogance — it's a prerequisite.
05
🌱
Impact Story
Who does this help? How does their life change because your product exists? Make us feel the impact — not just understand it intellectually.
06
🤝
Team Energy
The team that built it matters as much as what they built. Passion is contagious. Clarity is rare. Bring both and the judges will remember you.
